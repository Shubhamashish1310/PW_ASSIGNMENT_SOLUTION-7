MS DHONI

>

MS Dhoni's Biggest Achievements

  • Captain of the Indian team that won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
  • Vice-captain of the Indian team that won the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in 2007.
  • Led India to victory in the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.
  • Led India to the No. 1 ranking in Test cricket for the first time in December 2009.
  • Led Chennai Super Kings (CSK) to multiple Indian Premier League (IPL) titles.
  • One of the most successful ODI captains in terms of matches won and series/tournament victories.
  • Known for his remarkable composure and decision-making abilities under pressure.
  • Exceptional wicketkeeper-batsman, known for his lightning-quick stumpings and safe hands behind the wickets.
  • Received the Padma Bhushan and Padma Shri, two of India's highest civilian awards.
  • Received the prestigious Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, India's highest honor for achievement in sports.
  • Named MTV Youth Icon of the Year in 2006.
  • Regularly featured in Forbes' list of the world's highest-paid athletes.
